What is Butter?

Butter is the result of many developers and designers putting a bunch of APIs together
to make the experience of watching torrent movies as simple as possible.
We are an open source project. We are from all over the world. We love our movies.
And boy, do we love Popcorn with Butter.

Be social and support us!
